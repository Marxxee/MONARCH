ABOUT MMC

Founded on September 16 2018, Monarch Model College is a forward-thinking school committed to academic excellence, moral integrity and preparing students for future success. We have remained devout to providing a well-rounded education that prepares students not only for academic excellence but also for life. In Monarch Model College, every child is nurtured to reach their full potential.


Vision Statement

The vision of MMC is to raise exemplary comunity empowering learners with the knowledge, skills and resources necessary for life. We honour achievement and promote pride in ourselves, in our school and in our community.

Mission Statement

The mission of MMC is to educate, empower and enable all students to become caring, contributing citizens who can succeed in an ever changing world.

Our Core Values

  • Excellence
  • Discipline
  • Integrity
  • Creativity
  • Teamwork

Our Facilities

Our school is equipped with:

  • Modern classrooms and science laboratories
  • ICT and e-learning facilities
  • A resourceful library
  • Sports and recreational ground
  • Safe, child-friendly spaces for growth and exploration

At Monarch Model College, parents, teachers and students form a close-knit community. Join us at MMC, where every child's journey is valued, and every achievement is celebrated. Together, we raise Kings and Queens!
